Adding a Task

  • Add a Timed Task                                                        
    • Type “add &title %date $time @location”


E.g. Typing “add &dinner with family %25/12/2015 $7pm-9pm @Home” is adding an event of “dinner with family” on the 25th of December 2015, at 7pm to 9pm at home.


  • Add a Floating Task                                                     
    • Type “add &title %date @location”


E.g. Typing “add &Bring the dogs to the vet %4/3/2015 @VetHouse” is adding a floating task event of “Bring the dogs to the vet” on the 4th of March 2015 at the VetHouse.


  • Add a Deadline                                                            
    • Type “add &title by &date $time @location”


    • E.g. Typing “add &Homework 5 due %5/5/2015 $23:59 @IVLE” is adding a deadline task of “Homework 5” due one the 5th of May 2015, at 11.59pm.
