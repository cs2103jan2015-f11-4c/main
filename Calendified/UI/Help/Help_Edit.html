Editing a Task

  • Editing a Timed Task                                                   
    • Type “edit <index of the Timed Task> <new information>”


E.g. An index of 2 having the event  of “Dinner with family on

the 25th of December 2015, at 7pm-9pm at home”. 


-To change the location:

-type “edit 2 @aunt’s house” will change the location

from “home” to “aunt’s house”.


-To change the date:

-Type “edit 2 %24/12/2015” will change the date from the

25th to 24th.


-To change the time:

-Type “edit 2 $6pm-8pm” will change the timing from

7pm-9pm to 6pm-8pm.


-To change the title:

-Type “edit 2 &Christmas Celebration!” will change the

title from “Dinner with family” to “Christmas Celebration!”.


Type “/back” to go back to the main page.
